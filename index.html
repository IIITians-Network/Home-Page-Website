Brief Intro About us


  • IIITians Network is an Indian online social media and social networking service especially for students of Indian Institutes of Information Technology. Our primary aim is to provide a better platform for the exchange of information between IIITs.
  • Besides this, we also focus on providing a better reach to the events organized by clubs/societies of different colleges. We are also trying to strengthen brand IIIT.
    Our website aims for providing better knowledge about IIITs to the audience.
    There are special tools for JEE Aspirants

Code Chronicles:
It is a prestigious event which is an outcome of collaboration of all IIIT's with IIITians Network. It's a coding platform offered to coders which urges them to showcase their coding skills and obtain exposure to competitive coding. The idea behind code chronicles is that we want you to learn while competing with IIITians who are known for their coding culture. It provides a platform for interaction, competition and discussion for the coders of IIITs and various other well known Engineering Institutions of India. It includes questions of different difficulty levels, which focus on fundamentals of coding.

COMET (Community of Engineer and Teachgeeks):

This is our, another step towards creating something productive for our community. Under this, we are going to make sub-communities of a specific domain (e.g. Web Devs, ML, etc.) and we ask students from all IIITs if they have some knowledge and interest in a certain domain then they could join that community where they could easily share their ideas among the students with the similar mindset. And in this way, they could build up some great projects and this was not possible before the establishment of the IIITians Network.

Benefits of joining network.


  • Connecting with IIITians Network is same as getting in touch with all the IIIT’s by being acknowledged with news, events and collaborations happening in the IIIT’s on a single platform, as we work to put forth the latest news and updates from all the IIIT’s. We are also sharing the valuable information like placements, academics, curriculum, cut-off, etc., thus providing you with the accurate knowledge about all the IIIT’s. The network not only provides you with the information but also enables you to escalate your creativity and visionary. As one can easily take part in the contests and challenges organized by us, or can gain an ample of interest in a particular topic by joining the webinars and other technical sessions hosted by us.
  • How we help college Clubs/Societies:
    The network helps clubs/societies by providing a better reach to their events so that they could get the maximum no. of participants. We are also working on providing separate spaces to them on our websites where they could publish updates regarding their work and events. That could be easily accessible to our audience.
